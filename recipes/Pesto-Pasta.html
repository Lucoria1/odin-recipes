Pesto Pasta

Description

Feast not just your eyes on a delicious recipe for a wonderful bowl of pasta!

Ingredients

  • 75g of your favourite type of pasta
  • A jar of the cheapest pesto you can find at the supermarket
  • 35g of cheese so mature it could be your grandmother
  • 2 slices of smokey, smokey bacon
  • 90g of some jones barbacue and foot massage chimken

Steps

  1. Boil yo wa'er
  2. slap dat pasta in
  3. tell echo to set a timer for 11 minutes
  4. chop up the bacon and chimken to your desired thackness
  5. when echo starts shouting, drain the pasta and set it aside
  6. cook the bacon and chimken in the pan
  7. throw the pasta back in and add to large teaspoons of pesto
  8. stir till its all mixed together
  9. add any seasonings you like, I recommend smoked paprika and black pepper
  10. plate up and top with cheese
  11. Enjoy!!
