Pizza

Description

A pizza that was totally not frozen and whacked into the oven

Ingredients

  • Frozen Pizza
  • Chopped Chicken
  • Chopped Bacon
  • Italian Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Pre-Heat oven to 200 degrees celsius
  2. Sprinkle italian seasonings onto your totally not frozen pizza
  3. Stick pizza in oven for 10 minutes
  4. After 10 minutes, add chicken and bacon to pizza and cover with cheese
  5. Return to oven for 5 minutes
  6. Remove, slice, and enjoy!
